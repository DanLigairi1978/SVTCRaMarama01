# SVTC Website — Feature Roadmap & ROI Proposal

**Prepared by:** Meridian Solutions (Fiji) Ltd  
**Date:** 19 February 2026  
**Client:** Soqosoqo Vakamarama iTaukei Cakaudrove (SVTC)

---

## Executive Summary

The SVTC website is now live and fully operational. To maximise its value, drive visitor engagement, and justify ongoing investment, we recommend the following 10 feature enhancements. Each feature is designed to save SVTC time, increase visibility, or generate revenue — delivering clear return on investment.

---

## Proposed Feature Enhancements

### 1. 📸 Photo Gallery / Story Wall

A visual gallery where SVTC members upload photos from village visits, workshops, and events. Each photo gets a caption and location tag — like Instagram for the organisation. Women love sharing their stories visually, and this alone could double return visits.

**Impact:** High engagement, increased return visits  
**Effort:** Medium

---

### 2. 🗳️ Online Membership Registration

A simple form where new members register, select their district/tikina, and receive a digital membership card (PDF). Replaces paper forms entirely and gives SVTC real data on membership numbers — invaluable for donor reports and grant applications.

**Impact:** Operational efficiency, data collection  
**Effort:** Low

---

### 3. 💰 Online Donations (Stripe / M-PAiSA)

A "Donate Now" button that accepts card payments or integrates with M-PAiSA (Vodafone Fiji's mobile money). Even small donations add up. Donors receive an automatic receipt email. This is the number one ROI feature.

**Impact:** Direct revenue generation  
**Effort:** Medium

---

### 4. 📰 Monthly Newsletter / Blog

A blog section where SVTC publishes monthly updates, success stories, and upcoming events. Each post is shareable on Facebook. Content drives traffic, traffic drives visibility, and visibility drives donor interest.

**Impact:** SEO, social sharing, donor engagement  
**Effort:** Low–Medium

---

### 5. 📅 Event Registration System

Instead of just listing events on the calendar, allow people to register online. SVTC gets a headcount before each event, attendee contact details, and can send reminder emails. Replaces phone trees and WhatsApp coordination.

**Impact:** Event planning efficiency  
**Effort:** Low–Medium

---

### 6. 🌍 Interactive Impact Map

A map of Cakaudrove province showing every village where SVTC has run programs. Clickable pins reveal what was done, how many women were reached, and a photo. Donors and government agencies respond strongly to visual impact data.

**Impact:** Donor confidence, grant applications  
**Effort:** Medium

---

### 7. 📊 Live Impact Dashboard (Public)

A public-facing page displaying real-time statistics: "487 women trained", "12 villages reached", "3 scholarships awarded". Numbers auto-update from the database. Creates a sense of momentum, accountability, and progress.

**Impact:** Transparency, donor trust  
**Effort:** Medium

---

### 8. 🎓 Online Training / Resource Library

Upload PDFs, videos, or simple guides — for example, "How to Start a Village Savings Group" or "Traditional Weaving Techniques". Members can access resources anytime from any device. Positions SVTC as a knowledge hub, not just an event organiser.

**Impact:** Member value, knowledge preservation  
**Effort:** Medium

---

### 9. 💬 Testimonials / Success Stories Carousel

A rotating section on the homepage where women share short quotes about how SVTC changed their lives. Real stories, real faces. Emotionally powerful for donors and visitors. Easy to update directly from the admin dashboard.

**Impact:** Emotional engagement, donor conversion  
**Effort:** Low

---

### 10. 📱 WhatsApp Integration / Push Notifications

A "Join our WhatsApp Group" button or a simple notification system that alerts members when new events or news are posted. Meets people where they already are — on their phones.

**Impact:** Member communication, engagement  
**Effort:** Low
